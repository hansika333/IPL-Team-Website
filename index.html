About RCB

Royal Challengers Bengaluru (RCB) is one of the most popular teams in the Indian Premier League (IPL). The franchise was established in 2008 and is based in Bengaluru. RCB is known for its passionate fan base, exciting performances, and memorable matches. The team plays its home matches at M. Chinnaswamy Stadium.
